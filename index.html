Benefits of Biotechnology in Food

Food biotechnology is the use of technology to modify the genes of our food sources which are animals, plants, and microorganisms for producing more, survive in tough condition & healthy.
Modifying plants, animals and microorganisms through methods of genetic modification that are being used to introduce diverse beneficial characteristics.

Genetically Modified Organisms(GMO)

GMO crops are crops that have been safely genetically engineered to have new traits such as increased vitamin availability, drought resistance and pest tolerance.These foods increasing yields, supporting conservation and building sustainability.Some common GMO food:

  • Corn
  • Soybean
  • Cotton
  • Potato
  • Brinjal
